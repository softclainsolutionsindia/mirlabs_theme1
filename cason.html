International Conference on Computational Aspects of Social Networks (CASoN) Conference Series

The International Conference on Computational Aspects of Social Networks (CASoN)  brings together an interdisciplinary venue for social scientists, mathematicians, computer scientists, engineers, computer users, and students to exchange and share their experiences, new ideas, and research results about all aspects (theory, applications and tools) of intelligent methods applied to Social Network, and to discuss the practical challenges encountered and the solutions adopted. Industrial Workshop/Tutorials and conference sessions  allow individuals interested in the theory, methods, or applications of social network analysis to share ideas and explore common interests.

The conference provides an opportunity for the researchers to meet and discuss the latest solutions, scientific results and methods in solving intriguing problems in the fields of Social Networks. The conference programme include workshops, special sessions and tutorials, along with prominent keynote speakers and regular paper presentations in parallel tracks.
