The European Conference on Data Mining (ECDM) Conference Series

The European Conference on Data Mining is aimed to gather researchers and application developers from a wide range of data mining related areas such as statistics, computational intelligence, pattern recognition, databases and visualization. ECDM is aimed to advance the state of the art in data mining field and its various real world applications. ECDM also provides opportunities for technical collaboration among data mining and machine learning researchers around the globe.
